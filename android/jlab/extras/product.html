      Product Description
      Featuring a host of innovative technologies and a massive storage space, this double-door Haier refrigerator helps you keep your food fresh, healthy and tasty for a long duration.
      1 Hour Icing Technology

      This technology helps in bringing the freezer temperature down to -5° C in about 60 minutes. As a result, the fridge makes ice cubes really fast.

      360° Air Flow

      Thanks to this innovative feature, cold air reaches every nook and cranny of the fridge, thus cooling it uniformly. In addition, this feature also helps the fridge maintain an optimum humidity level. It also ensures that there is no air blockage. All this helps in taking the pressure off the compressor, so it runs with less wear and tear.

      Stabilizer-free Operation

      If your area faces a lot of voltage fluctuations, you don’t need to worry about it affecting your fridge’s operations. This fridge can operate safely and stably within a range of 135 - 290 V.

      Child Lock

      Since this fridge comes with a child lock, it prevents your kids from sneaking out food behind your back.
